    Frequently Asked Questions

    How quickly can I get started with the Family Budget Planner?

    You can create your account and start using our platform immediately. The initial setup takes about 10 minutes, and you'll be tracking expenses and setting goals right away. Our onboarding guide walks you through every step.

    Is my financial data secure?

    Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, secure data centers, and multi-factor authentication to protect your information. We never sell your data to third parties and maintain strict privacy policies.

    Can multiple family members access the same account?

    Yes! Our platform supports multiple user accounts with customizable permission levels. Parents can maintain oversight while giving appropriate access to children and teens, making it perfect for teaching financial literacy.

    What if I need help using the platform?

    We offer comprehensive support through multiple channels: email support, phone assistance during business hours, detailed help documentation, video tutorials, and an active community forum where families share tips and advice.
